About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science at Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University (XJTLU). My research interests lie in Recommender Systems, Generative Recommendation, and Large Language Models (LLMs). I am particularly focused on building end-to-end generative frameworks that integrate semantic tokenization, reasoning, and controllable generation for intelligent recommendation.

Research Interests

  • Generative Recommender Systems (e.g., TIGER, OneRec, EAGER)
  • Latent Variable Models and RQ-VAE for Semantic Tokenization
  • LLM-based Recommendation and Reasoning Alignment
  • Contrastive Learning and Cross-domain Representation Alignment

Projects

  • RGRG: Report Generation and Region Guidance for medical imaging, combining vision-language modeling and controllable report synthesis.
